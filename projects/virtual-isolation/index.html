Visual Isolation

Visual Isolation is a wearable interactive object equipped with a display that shows real-time vital readings and memories. Our lives have been dominated by numbers and graphs for quite some time. Society is attempting to navigate a new relationship between our survival and our social interactions during periods of isolation, yet the future remains uncertain. Despite human memory being deeply dependent on our social interactions, we find ourselves caught between fear and the loss of social abilities. "Is surviving alone enough to stay human, and what remains without shared memories?" - this question inspired the development of this project.
